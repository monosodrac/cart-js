About

Polyphia was founded in 2010 in Plano, Texas. Initially, the band’s sound leaned heavily into instrumental metalcore with progressive influences. Their debut EP, "Inspire," released in 2013, captured this early style. The EP gained significant attention, particularly through a guitar playthrough of the song "Impassion" on YouTube, which racked up over a million views and brought Polpyhia widespread recognition within the metal and progressive rock community.

At first, Polyphia embarked on the traditional route of touring extensively, however, the band soon realized that touring wasn’t as fruitful for them as other promotional avenues. Instead, they shifted their focus toward digital marketing and YouTube content, where their intricate guitar work and innovative sound gained traction. This pivot proved to be essential to their early success, allowing the band to build a fanbase without the financial strain of touring.
